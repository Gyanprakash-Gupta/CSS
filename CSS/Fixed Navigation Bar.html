E-mail stands for electronic mail. It is one of the most commonly used services on the Internet. It allows people to send messages to one or more recipients. On October 29th 1969, the first message was sent from computer to computer on ARPANET.


But complete email service was used in year 1971 by Ray Tomlinson(inventor of electronic mail). Examples of some email service provider companies are: Gmail,Outlook,Yahoo! Mail etc.


The speed at which an email can be sent and received is a huge benefit to most people. People can communicate in minutes or seconds from anywhere in the world.

Emails can be faster and easier than a phone call, as there's no danger of being put on hold or forced to engage in lengthy conversations.


It's simple to attach any file to an email message.


Email accounts are like large folders for not only private messages but also files and other important information. Good email clients make it easy to organize, archive, and search through your messages.


Most email providers give us free access to an email account. We can send and receive all the electronic mail we want, and store everything online without ever paying a rupee.


Email contents are mainly classified as two parts, the header and the body.


The email header gives common details about the message. The details of sender and receiver are stored here.


This field specifies the sender’s email address. Usually, it will be the address to which reply will be sent.


The actual content is stored in this part. This will be in the format of text. This field could also include signatures or text generated automatically by the sender’s email system.
